Trash Hoops- DESIGN QUEST

Our teams design quest consists of a variety of minigames that each show a different sociatal problem given as choices. These games consist of Trash Hoops, Safe Driving, Healthy & Hungry, Energy Quest and The Water Conservation Mission. Each of these games can help to spread awarness about their areas of concern. These fun 5-minute experiences can educate and give you a new outlook on certain issues.

Trash Hoops

Trash hoop, a fun, quick mini-game with an education side. Students or children can play to find out and understand what trash goes in which bin. It can either be compost, recycle, or trash. This game is filled with fun, check it out!

Safe Driving!

This Minigame focuses on the sociatal issue of safe transportation. Around 1.2 million die due to car realted accidents annualy. A lot of lives can be saved if we just bring the importance of practicing safe driving into the public eye. This minigame strives to do just that. In this game the player is in control of a car and has to abide by the various traffic rules in order to retain their score and avoid angering the traffic cop. We hope that through games like this we can bring awarness to the populace about important topics such as these.

Solar Conservation

An awesome game packed with scenery and gameplay. The game focuses on promoting change in the way we get our energy. This game is meant to benefit and educate the new generations in a quick easy way! Have fun!
